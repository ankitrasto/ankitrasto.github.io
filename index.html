About Me


Hello! I am a family physician from Toronto with an interest in quality improvement, information security and open source software. This is my landing page for projects that I am involved with in or outside the medical field. Currently, I maintain two software projects for research in the areas of air quality, lipidomics and statistical modelling and am a contributor to a project in health IT. If I'm not doing any of this stuff I can be probably found cycling or taking photos.

Projects

Librehealth Radiology Module

Joined: 2016

A open-source module that interfaces with librehealth EHR to improve the workflow of EHR for radiologists by providing a robust radiology information system framework. I collaborate to develop user-stories for the project to guide its development and perform beta-testing.

AETMS - Electronic Temperature Monitoring

Started: 2015

A hardware and software solution which logs temperature information, is visually representable via a web-interface and improves on existing implementations by notifying users through twitter/email.

MetCor - Statistical Modelling

Started: 2010 (maintenance phase)

Software for large-scale novel statistical modelling with meteorological HYSPLIT trajectories and pollution data. Models include CWT, QTBA, PSCF and recursive models.

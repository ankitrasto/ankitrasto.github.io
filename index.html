About Me

Hello! I am a Family Medicine resident at the University of Toronto with an interest in quality improvement, information security and open source software. This is my landing page and a porfolio page for my software projects in or outside the medical field. Currently, I maintain two projects for research in the areas of air quality, lipidomics and statistical modelling. If I'm not doing any of this stuff I can be probably found cycling.

Portfolio and Projects

AETMS - Electronic Temperature Monitoring

Started: 2015 (development phase)

A hardware and software solution which logs temperature information, is visually representable via a web-interface and improves on existing implementations by notifying users through twitter/email.

raeLipID/RKMD - Lipidomics

Started: 2013 (development phase)

Software for high throughput lipid screening using experimental mass data in conjunction with relative kendrick mass defects and other criteria.

View Project

MetCor - Statistical Modelling

Started: 2010 (maintenance phase)

Software for large-scale novel statistical modelling with meteorological HYSPLIT trajectories and pollution data. Models include CWT, QTBA, PSCF and recursive models.
